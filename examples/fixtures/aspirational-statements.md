# Aspirational Statements

### ASP-001

- linked_statement: P-04.SS-01
- claim: ALR-217 maintains response durability in r/r DLBCL through extended follow-up, supporting its use as a durable oral option in 3L+ where treatment continuity is operationally important.
- rationale: Durability is a primary differentiation axis against bispecific antibodies. The current data cut supports response rate but is insufficient for durability claims of clinical relevance. The ongoing long-term follow-up readout is expected to either substantiate or constrain this claim. Holding it as an aspirational statement now preserves the strategic narrative while honestly representing the evidentiary state.
- evidence_generation_link: long-term follow-up readout from the pivotal Phase 2 (anticipated input recorded in the roadmap)
- expected_readout: tbd
- fallback_position: If long-term follow-up does not support durability claims of clinical relevance, frame ALR-217's evidence as response-rate-anchored at the time of platform development; do not claim durability beyond what the readout supports. Re-position the differentiation argument toward convenience, sequencing flexibility, and tolerability — Strategic Priority 1 — rather than durability.
