# Roadmap

## Engagement Summary

Build a Scientific Communication Platform for ALR-217 (oral small-molecule inhibitor) in relapsed/refractory DLBCL, supporting pre-launch communication to hematologist-oncologists, academic KOLs, and P&T committees. Anticipated launch is approximately 18 months out; the platform must be ready to support medical-affairs activities through that window.

## Strategic Priorities

1. Differentiate ALR-217 from emerging bispecific antibodies on convenience, sequencing flexibility, and tolerability in older patients.
2. Establish optimal positioning in the 3rd-line and beyond setting given the post-CAR-T treatment landscape.
3. Communicate the safety profile credibly to community oncologists, including practical management of expected adverse events.

## Status

In briefing sprint (Sprint 1). Knowledge-base seeded with pivotal Phase 2 trial publication, mechanism-of-action paper, two competitor publications, NCCN guideline excerpt, ASH abstract on elderly subgroup, and review article on post-CAR-T sequencing. Pillar set not yet proposed.
