# Fact-Check Report

## Scope

P-04 (Clinical Evidence) pillar in full: SS-01 with its two reference statements (RS-01 overall response rate, RS-02 elderly subgroup), evaluated against the cited sources REF-001 (pivotal Phase 2 publication) and REF-005 (ASH elderly subgroup abstract). Operating context: drafting.

## Findings

### FC-04-001

- target: P-04.SS-01.RS-02
- issue: RS-02 cites both REF-001 and REF-005 for elderly-subgroup efficacy, but REF-001's elderly-subgroup data are reported only as a brief mention with limited detail; the substantive subgroup findings are in REF-005, which is a congress abstract (lower source strength than peer-reviewed publication). Source strength is asymmetric to claim weight.
- severity: medium
- recommendation: Either soften RS-02's wording to better match the strength of REF-005 (congress abstract framing), or open an evidence gap (linked to RS-02) for a peer-reviewed elderly-subgroup publication. Note: GAP-001 already tracks this — confirm linkage.

### FC-04-002

- target: P-04.SS-01.RS-01
- issue: RS-01 references "the protocol-defined primary analysis after a median follow-up of N months" but does not specify whether N is from REF-001 or a more recent data cut. Confirm the N value and the data cut date match REF-001 specifically.
- severity: low
- recommendation: Verify the median follow-up in REF-001 and populate N explicitly, or qualify with "as reported at primary analysis."
