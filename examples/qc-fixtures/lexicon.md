# Lexicon

### diffuse large B-cell lymphoma

- preferred: diffuse large B-cell lymphoma (DLBCL) on first use; DLBCL thereafter
- avoid: [diffuse large b-cell lymphoma, dlbcl (lowercase), DLCL]
- definition: An aggressive non-Hodgkin lymphoma of B-cell origin.
- rationale: NCCN-aligned orthography. Capitalization of "B-cell" with hyphenated lowercase "cell" is conventional in hematology literature.

### relapsed/refractory

- preferred: relapsed/refractory (r/r) on first use; r/r thereafter
- avoid: [relapsed and refractory, R/R]
- definition: Disease that has either recurred after prior response (relapsed) or never responded to / progressed during prior therapy (refractory).
- rationale: Slash-separated lowercase "r/r" matches majority hematology convention.
