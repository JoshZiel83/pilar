# Style Guide

## Voice and Tone

Dry scientific register. Test fixture; minimal content.

## Sentence Construction

Default to medium-length declarative sentences. No emphasis devices.

## Disallowed Patterns

The following patterns are disallowed by default in pilar drafted copy. They codify the dry-scientific-register requirements in §9 of the natural-language spec.

1. **Em dashes used for rhythmic effect.** Permitted only for genuine parenthetical insertions.
2. **Antithetical constructions of the form "it is not X, it is Y".** Disallowed regardless of factual accuracy. The construction is rhetorical, not scientific.
3. **Sentence-initial conjunctions used for rhetorical pacing.** Internal conjunctions are unaffected.
4. **Short punchy sentences used for emphasis.**
5. **Hedging phrases that soften factual claims without scientific reason.**
6. **Marketing-register adjectives.** "Robust," "compelling," "groundbreaking," "transformative," etc.
7. **First-person plural framing of the science.** "We see…", "Our data show…"
8. **Rhetorical questions.**

### Overrides

No overrides for this fixture.

## Citation Conventions

Vancouver-style for the test fixture.

## Evidence Description Conventions

Single-arm trial findings reported with point estimates and confidence intervals; comparative claims framed as cross-trial descriptive comparisons.

## Other

None.
